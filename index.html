Don Toliver

Biography

Caleb Zackery Toliver(born June 12, 1994), known professionally as Don Toliver, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He released his first major label mixtape, Donny Womack, in August 2018. He is known for his singles No Idea and After Party, from his debut studio album, Heaven or Hell(2020), which gained popularity on the video-sharing app TikTok, as well as his August 2018 feature label-boss Travis Scott's song Can't Say, from his third studio album Astroworld. In August 2020, he was featured alongside Nav on the song Lemonade by Internet Money and Gunna.
